# Tropical Mango

A fruity tropical flavor with a smooth mango-forward taste.

![Tropical Mango](/images/tropical-mango-image.png)

---

## Flavor Profile

**Taste:** Tropical, sweet, fruity, and refreshing.

**Best For:** People who like fruit-flavored energy drinks without a heavy sour taste.

---

## What It Tastes Like

Tropical Mango is a Ghost Energy flavor made for people who want something more exciting than a basic energy drink. It has a strong personality and fits the bold, neon style of the Ghost brand.

---

## Flavor Notes

- Mango gives it a smooth tropical base.
- Refreshing when served ice cold.
- Good choice for someone who wants a fruit flavor instead of candy flavor.

---

## Quick Rating

This flavor is a good choice when you want something cold, flavorful, and high-energy. It works well for studying, gaming, working out, or just replacing a regular soda-style drink.
