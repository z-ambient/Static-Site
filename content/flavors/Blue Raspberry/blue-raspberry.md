## Flavor Profile

**Taste:** Sweet, sour, tangy, and bright.

**Best For:** People who like sour candy flavors and want something more intense than a basic berry drink.

## What It Tastes Like

Sour Patch Kids Blue Raspberry is a Ghost Energy flavor made for people who want something more exciting than a basic energy drink. It has a strong personality and fits the bold, neon style of the Ghost brand.

- Finishes with a sweet blue raspberry flavor.
- One of the most recognizable candy-inspired Ghost flavors.
